Lisa Santonato

Lisa Santonato is a writer, artist, and filmmaker with twenty+ years in digital media and film/video production. A highly organized, detail-oriented, passionate, and committed Associate Producer, Lisa brings to her clients a diverse skill set having directed, produced, and delivered a wide range of online/digital media content over the past two decades.

Her key strengths and areas of expertise in storytelling include writing, directing, shooting, editing, and photography. She is currently developing new skills in data analytics, research, and visualization, as well as front end web development, through the Data Analytics Boot Camp at the University of Toronto's School of Continuing Studies.
